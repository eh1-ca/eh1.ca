A Social Network to be Made for Canadians, by Canadians!

A place where We as Canadians can connect, share, and express freely, while staying true to our values of privacy, fairness, and respect.

What Makes Us Different?

  • You own your content. Unlike other platforms, we don’t claim ownership of what you post. You create it, you control it. We’re just here to host it.
  • No tracking. No AI farming. No hidden agendas. Your data stays yours, and we don’t sell it or use it without your explicit permission.
  • Built to match Canadian law. Free speech within legal bounds, full privacy protections, and a community that respects Canadian values.

No Ads, No Middlemen

  • We won’t inject ads into your feed.
  • Users can choose to post promotional content—but only with full transparency and compliance with Canadian ad laws.
  • Your feed is about what you want to see, not what an algorithm thinks you should.

Privacy First, Always

  • No invasive tracking, no personal data analysis.
  • We take security seriously—our job is to protect your space, not exploit it.
  • Any future features will be opt-in, never automatic.

A True Canadian Experience

  • Our platform speaks both English & French—because so do we.
  • Community-driven standards: Be nice, be fair, be Canadian.
  • Whether you’re an artist, a writer, a business, or just someone who loves sharing their thoughts—this space is for you fellow Canadian.
