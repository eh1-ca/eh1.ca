Why not?

Eh! is a place where community comes first.
That is our commitment, our core value.
Everything else is secondary.

How?

  • No Ads. Yes, you read it right—we won’t inject ads into your feed.
  • No tracking. No AI farming. No hidden agendas. Your data stays yours—we just host it.
  • Donations—because we’re not here for profit, we’re here for the people.

Privacy First, Always

  • No metadata, no behaviour tracking, no profiling—we don’t track, store, or sell your data.
  • Any future features always will be opt-in, never opt-out.
  • We take security seriously—our job is to protect our space, not exploit it.

Community

  • Our platform speaks both English & French—because so do we.
  • Community-driven standards: Be nice, be fair, be Canadian.
  • Whether you’re an artist, a writer, a business, or just someone who loves sharing their thoughts—this space is for you.
